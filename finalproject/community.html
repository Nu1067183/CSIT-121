Community Outreach and Volunteering

I had the opportunity to volunteer in various activities organized through my school, which allowed me to engage directly with the community and promote oral health awareness. These experiences were both rewarding and educational, helping me strengthen my communication skills and deepen my understanding of public health. Additionally, I completed an on-site rotation at the University of Maryland Rehabilitation Dentistry, where I gained valuable hands-on experience working with patients who have complex medical and physical needs. This externship further enhanced my ability to provide compassionate, patient-centered care in diverse settings.
