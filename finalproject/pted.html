Patient Education Materials

During my first year in the dental hygiene program, I created a brochure to help educate patients on the important connection between nutrition and oral health. This brochure highlights how dietary choices can influence oral hygiene, such as the impact of sugary foods on tooth decay and the role of vitamins and minerals in maintaining healthy gums and teeth. It was a valuable project that allowed me to apply my knowledge and communicate essential health information to patients in an accessible way. You can view the brochure below.



In addition, I developed an AV aid aimed at educating adolescents on the proper technique for brushing their teeth. This tool is designed to help them understand the importance of effective brushing and encourage the development of lifelong oral hygiene habits for better dental health.
