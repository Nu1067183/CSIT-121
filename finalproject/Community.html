Community Outreach and Volunteering

I had the opportunity to volunteer in activities organized through my school. These experiences allowed me to engage with the community and promote oral health awareness.My involvement in these events has been both rewarding and educational.

Kids-Smile Spectacular 2023

I had the opportunity to contribute by decorating the day before the event, setting up operatories, and assisting clinician students during the event by sterilizing instruments and maintaining a smooth workflow. This experience gave me valuable insights into teamwork and the importance of preparation in providing excellent patient care.

Deaf and Dental 2024

I had the privilege of working alongside interpreting students to provide preventive dental services to members of the deaf community. This experience helped me develop a deeper understanding of the unique needs of diverse patient populations and the importance of fostering inclusive care environments.
